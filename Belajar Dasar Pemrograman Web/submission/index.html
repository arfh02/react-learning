Biografi

Tipnaree Weerawatnodom, nicknamed Namtan, is a Thai actress managed by GMM TV. Raised in Ayutthaya province, she graduated from the Faculty of Fine Arts—majoring in acting and directing—from Srinakharinwirot University. In 2024, Namtan graduated with a Master Degree in Performing Arts in the same Faculty.

he is the younger sister of Thai professional footballer Narubadin Weerawatnodom (Ton).

From 2013 to 2015, she was a well-known host of the show "Strawberry Krubcake." For her role in "Who Are You" (2020) playing twin sisters, Tipnaree was nominated as "Best Actress in a Leading Role" in the 25th "Asian Television Awards" (2021).

Outside her acting career, Tipnaree has an online shop of fish chili paste called Nam-Prik-Ta-Lood (IG: aroy_talood).
